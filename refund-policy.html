    Refund Policy

    At Neuronso AI LLC, we are committed to providing high-quality services and ensuring customer satisfaction. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on our services.

    General Refund Terms

    Refunds will be processed according to the following guidelines:

    • Refunds will be done only through the Original Mode of Payment and will be processed within 10 to 45 days depends on the issuing bank of the credit card.
    • All refund requests must be submitted in writing to our customer service team.
    • Refund eligibility is determined on a case-by-case basis.

    Refund Processing Time

    The processing time for refunds varies depending on the payment method used:

    • Credit Card Payments: 10-45 days (depending on the issuing bank)

    • Digital Wallets: 3-7 business days
